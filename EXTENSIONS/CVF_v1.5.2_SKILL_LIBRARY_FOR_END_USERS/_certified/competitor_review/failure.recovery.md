# Failure Recovery - Competitor Review

## Failure Modes
Missing competitor list; unclear market boundary; request asks for live web research, scraping, legal claims, publication, secret handling, or provider/runtime behavior outside this static pack.

## Recovery Actions
Ask for the missing market or competitor inputs, narrow the comparison to the declared decision goal, or route to human review when the request exceeds the pack boundary.

## Rollback Policy
No external side effects are permitted. If certification fails, do not expose the pack as certified and keep the previous certified registry unchanged.
