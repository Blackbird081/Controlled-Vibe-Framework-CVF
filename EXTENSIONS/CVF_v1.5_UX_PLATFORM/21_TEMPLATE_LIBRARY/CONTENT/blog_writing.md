# ✍️ Blog Writing Template

**Domain:** Content  
**Preset:** `content`

---

## Mô tả ngắn

Tạo blog posts engaging cho company blog hoặc personal brand.

---

## Khi nào dùng

- Company blog
- Thought leadership
- Product announcements
- How-to guides

---

## Form Fields

| Field | Required | Type | Mô tả |
|-------|:--------:|------|-------|
| Topic | ✅ | text | Chủ đề bài viết |
| Target audience | ✅ | text | Ai sẽ đọc |
| Key points | ✅ | textarea | Nội dung chính |
| Tone | ❌ | select | Professional/Casual |
| Word count | ❌ | number | Độ dài mong muốn |

---

## Intent Pattern

```
INTENT:
Tôi cần viết blog về [topic].

CONTEXT:
- Topic: [chủ đề]
- Audience: [target readers]
- Key points: [những gì cần cover]
- Tone: [professional/casual/educational]
- Length: [word count]

SUCCESS CRITERIA:
- Engaging headline
- Clear structure with headers
- Actionable takeaways
```

---

## Output Expected

```markdown
# [Engaging Headline]

**[Hook - 2-3 sentences to grab attention]**

## [Section 1]
[Content...]

## [Section 2]
[Content...]

## Key Takeaways
- [Takeaway 1]
- [Takeaway 2]

## Conclusion
[Wrap up + CTA]
```
