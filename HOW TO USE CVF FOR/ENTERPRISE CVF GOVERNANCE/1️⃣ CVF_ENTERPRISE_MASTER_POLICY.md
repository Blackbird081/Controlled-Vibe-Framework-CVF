# CVF ENTERPRISE MASTER POLICY

## 1. PURPOSE

This document defines enterprise-wide governance
for all AI agents operating under Controlled Vibe Framework (CVF).

No agent may operate outside this policy.

---

## 2. SCOPE

Applies to:

- All internal AI agents
- All environments (dev, staging, production)
- All CVF versions
- All skill libraries

---

## 3. GOVERNANCE PRINCIPLES

1. No agent without registry entry.
2. No production without Self-UAT PASS.
3. No certification without audit.
4. No operation without risk classification.
5. No version change without re-validation.

---

## 4. AUTHORITY STRUCTURE

- Governance Board (final authority)
- CVF Architect
- Agent Owner
- Operator
- Auditor

Separation of Duties is mandatory.

---

## 5. RISK TIERS

LOW  
MEDIUM  
HIGH  
CRITICAL  

CRITICAL requires dual human approval.

---

## 6. ENFORCEMENT RULE

Violation of this policy results in:

- Immediate BLOCK
- Certification suspension
- Mandatory audit review
