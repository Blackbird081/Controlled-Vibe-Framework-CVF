# CVF RISK MATRIX

## RISK LEVEL DEFINITIONS

### LOW
Internal analysis only.
No external impact.

**Approval:**
- SME/Internal: Agent Owner
- Enterprise: Owner approval

---

### MEDIUM
Affects internal workflow or reporting.

**Approval:**
- SME/Internal: Owner + IT
- Enterprise: Owner + Architect

---

### HIGH
Impacts cost decisions, contracts, or customers.

**Approval:**
- SME/Internal: Department Manager
- Enterprise: Governance Board

---

### CRITICAL
Legal, compliance, or major financial exposure.

**Approval:**
- SME/Internal: Executive level
- Enterprise: Dual board approval

---
